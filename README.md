# Human Web
Human Web is meant to be a breath of fresh air from the AI dystopian world corporates are trying to create, the Internet was meant to be used by us humans, not by generative AI models.

Human Web is an open-source cyberspace free of hate and free of any kind of AI generated content, it's not a basic social network, it's a cyber-space, where you're not valued by the quality of your works yet by the journey that you've went through, a platform where you must be proud of your works because it is human.

Human Web doesn't ask you for money, nor it has paid subscriptions, it's kept up by voluntary donations and the love of humans of the internet always wishing for a better internet.

Human Web is not challenging any corporate.

Human Web is meant to be a comforting place for the people experiencing the emptiness of the [Generative AI depression](https://medium.com/@gabrielemidulla/generative-ai-and-the-great-depression-5a5790f9c7dd)
